Introduction¶

In this analysis we are trying to analyse the factors behind why patients do not show for appointments. The dataset "noshowappointments" collects information from 100k medical appointments in Brazil and is focused on the question of whether or not patients show up for their appointment.

Conclusions¶

From the analysis, I observe that the factors Age, Hipertension, Diabetes and Hadicap have an impact on no-show. Patients with the average Age of 37 years have less no-shows that the patients with the average Age of 34. Similarly patients suffering from Hipertension, Diabetes or are Handicap have comparitively less amount of no-shows. Factors like Gender, Alcoholism have no impact on no-show. Patients having Scholarship or received SMS have more percentage of no-shows.
